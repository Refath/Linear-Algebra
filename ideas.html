Book

At the conclusion of every Chapter -- which I consider a milestone --
I will write a reflection of sorts -- a culmination of all the central ideas
in retrospect. Learning by looking back, I believe -- is just as crucial as
learning by looking forward. Today, as of 4/30/20, I am on Chapter 2. You can read my
ideas below.

Note: At the conclusion of every Chapter, I reflect on the ideas I had -- some fruitful, some not. For instance, up until now, I've thought about definining the derivative parametrically, so as to use it in higher dimensions and multivariable functions; I've thought about determinants and almost clicked them with the Shoelace Theorem (a personal favorite of mine). And now, as I write this very note, I'm thinking of approaching a rotation on the plane through two approaches: The Rotation Matrix and Complex Numbers. Perhaps there is a way, even, that I can connect these two bodies of mathematics. There is much that awaits. ~ Refath Bari
