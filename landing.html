Chapter 1: Understanding Vectors

We really understand the idea of vectors and dot products. Standard vector operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication by scalar) are defined. We also understand the idea of linear combinations, vector spans, direction cosines and the relationship between Vectors and Matrices.

Chapter 2: Solving Linear Equations

Absolutely crucial chapter. We explore Gaussian Elimination to solve systems of linear equations, Gauss-Jordan Elimination to find Matrix Inverses, Elementary, Symmetric, Elimination, Permutation, and Identity Matrices. We turn reality on its head with the idea of treating functions as vectors, and intuitively understand the idea using Spike Diagrams. We look at vector spaces and much, much more.
